# Evidence Pack: MRV System (National Framework + Pilot Implementation Context)

## Chapter Purpose (for later drafting)
This pack captures the MRV system’s documented architecture, indicators, governance safeguards, and digital workflow while maintaining scope authority boundaries.  
It supports chapter drafting on how monitoring is designed to operate nationally and where pilot implementation depth is currently strongest.

## Core Findings (traceable)
- MRV report frames a **national blue carbon MRV framework** informed by baseline studies (extent, carbon, ecosystem health, pressures, governance, socio-economic). (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “1. STRATEGIC CONTEXT AND PURPOSE”).
- Institutional architecture is four-tier: community, district, national technical, and independent review (where required). (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “2.1 Institutional Tiers”).
- Functional layers: ecological/carbon integrity, environmental pressure surveillance, and governance/safeguard assurance. (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “2.2 Functional Layers”).
- Baseline reference logic in MRV specifies 2025 baseline package as Year 0 anchor for future benchmarking. (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “4.2.1 Baseline Reference”).
- Carbon monitoring design includes core pools (AGB, BGB, deadwood where relevant, SOC 0-100 cm), PSP registry, and remeasurement cycles (biomass every 3-5 years). (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “4.4 Carbon Stock Monitoring and Accounting”; “4.4.3 Re-Measurement Cycle”).
- Pressure surveillance specifies five core indicators (land conversion, mangrove cutting signal, fuel source shift, charcoal hotspot tracking, conflict/access pressure index) and quarterly/annual cadences. (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “5.3 Core Environmental Pressure Indicators”).
- Governance/safeguard indicator design includes female representation target (minimum 40% in executive leadership), grievance tracking, and benefit-sharing transparency documentation. (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “6.3 Institutional Representation Monitoring”; “6.5 Grievance Redress Mechanism”).
- Digital platform design includes weekly satellite updates, change alerts, role-based access, and integrated community->district->national data flow. (Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md` — “7. DIGITAL MONITORING AND WEB-BASED SPATIAL PLATFORM”).
- Scope logic note requires distinguishing MRV framework (national) from pilot implementation narrative (Keta has most complete layered package). (Source: `project_scope_logic.md` — “How the scope evolved”; “CRITICAL DISTINCTION”).

## Approved Numbers, Named Sites, Dates, Definitions
### Approved numbers and frequencies
- Minimum female representation target: 40% in executive leadership (MRV governance requirement).
- Biomass remeasurement interval: every 3-5 years.
- Soil carbon pool depth reference: 0-100 cm.
- Fuel-shift sample guidance: 20-30 households per CREMA annually.
- Monitoring frequencies: quarterly (multiple field indicators), annual (satellite extent and governance summaries), weekly (satellite feed updates).
(Source: `blue_carbon_mrv_report.md`, Sections 4-7).
