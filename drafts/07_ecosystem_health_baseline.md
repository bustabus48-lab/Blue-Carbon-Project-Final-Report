## 7.1 Baseline Position

This chapter is intentionally partial because core ecosystem-health source extraction remains incomplete.

## 7.2 Site-Differentiated Condition Narrative
Available evidence supports the following high-level pattern:
- one landscape with cumulative, distributed pressure linked to livelihood dependence and associated ecological stress;
- one landscape with strong conflict/extraction dynamics tied to land-use competition;
- one landscape where degraded buffers and transitional land pressure coexist with culturally protected pockets.

This framing is descriptive and cautious; it does not substitute for pending quantified health metrics.

## 7.3 Monitoring-Relevant Indicators in Current Sources

These components support readiness framing for ongoing monitoring but should not be presented as long-run trend results.
